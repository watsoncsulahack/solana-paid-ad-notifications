## Key reactions captured
- The concept is compelling because it inverts spam economics: notifications become paid access, not free extraction.
- The strongest narrative is **recipient control + advertiser cost**.
- The demo should emphasize practical execution over theoretical maximalism.

2. Make user control explicit (opt-in, limits, category preferences).
3. Keep Solana central where it matters: payment proof.
4. Avoid over-expanding scope before proving core mechanic.

The podcast also raised a meaningful concern: if attention is monetized, users can feel pressured to convert all time into inventory. MVP guidance now includes explicit attention safety controls (quiet hours, limits, dismiss/block, clear opt-in).
